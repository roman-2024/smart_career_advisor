## Project Title:
**Smart Career Advisor: AI-Powered Career Prediction and Guidance System**

---

## Abstract

Choosing the right career path is one of the most critical decisions in a student's life. However, due to a lack of guidance, exposure, or access to professional advice, many students find themselves confused about their future.  
To address this gap, we present **Smart Career Advisor** ‚Äî an AI-powered intelligent web application that recommends the most suitable career paths for individuals based on their skills, education, experiences, and interests.  

By leveraging machine learning algorithms and an interactive Streamlit-based UI, our system not only predicts ideal careers but also offers structured roadmaps and learning institution recommendations to help users move confidently towards their goals.

---

## Objectives

- To develop a user-friendly web application that predicts career paths based on personal attributes.
- To provide actionable career roadmaps with step-by-step skill development plans.
- To recommend institutions where students can further enhance their skills.
- To create an early career awareness platform for students and young professionals.

---

## Methodology

- **Data Collection:** We curated data covering various career domains, required skills, degree backgrounds, and experience levels.
- **Machine Learning Model:** A Logistic Regression model was trained to classify users into the most probable career paths.
- **Feature Engineering:** Skills, degrees, experiences, interests, and other demographic features were encoded and utilized.
- **Model Deployment:** The trained model was serialized using Pickle and deployed using Streamlit, ensuring accessibility and interactivity.

---

## Technologies Used

- **Python**
- **Scikit-learn** (Machine Learning)
- **Streamlit** (Web Application Framework)
- **Pickle** (Model Serialization)

## How It Benefits Students

- **Personalized Guidance:** Students receive customized career suggestions tailored to their own skills and interests.
- **Structured Learning Path:** They gain a clear understanding of the steps needed to enter and excel in a particular career.
- **Early Career Awareness:** Students in high school or university can plan their education and skill development more strategically.
- **Confidence Building:** By following a guided roadmap, students feel more confident in pursuing their goals.
